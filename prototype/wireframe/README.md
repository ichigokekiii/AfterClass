# After Class — Wireframe

Grayscale, structure-only mockup for the MVP student flow.

## Open the wireframe

```bash
open prototype/wireframe/index.html
```

Or drag `index.html` into any browser.

## Navigate

- Use **Prev / Next** at the bottom, or the dropdown to jump between screens.
- Tap buttons inside the phone frame to follow the happy path.

## Screens (15)

1. Welcome  
2. Age gate  
3. School email  
4. Verify email  
5. Profile setup  
6. Area and radius  
7. Discovery / swipe  
8. Match  
9. **Meetup proposal** (hero — countdown, no chat)  
10. Meetup confirmed  
11. Post-meetup review  
12. Thanks  
13. Chat (unlocked, demo)  
14. Match expired  
15. No shared venue (edge)

Product copy and rules are documented in `docs/after-class-prd.md` under **UX Flow and Wireframes**.
